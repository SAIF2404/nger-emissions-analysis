# NGER Emissions Analysis

## Project Overview
Analysis of Australia's corporate greenhouse gas emissions 
using the Clean Energy Regulator's published National 
Greenhouse and Energy Reporting (NGER) data.

## Tools Used
- SQLite — database design and management
- SQL — 15 queries including JOINs, subqueries 
  and window functions
- Power BI — 4 page interactive dashboard

## Key Findings
1. Australia's top 20 corporations produce 65.4% 
   of national Scope 1 emissions
2. National emissions declining — 307M to 298M 
   tonnes over three years
3. Energy sector dominates — 65% of top 20 emissions
4. Oil and Gas declining fastest — down 3M tonnes
5. Transport rising post-COVID — up 1.5M tonnes
6. Pechiney Consolidated — 83% Scope 2 share — 
   highest renewable transition opportunity

## Data Source
Clean Energy Regulator — NGER Corporate Emissions Data
https://cer.gov.au
